Headfort School

rebranding, art direction, logo creation, user experience, web design, user interface, custom WP site-build, product photography collateral, marketing material

Headfort School is a private boarding school boasting “an education that lasts a lifetime”. I was approached by the school to incorporate their new ethos of “Learn, Play Thrive” into a brand redesign while still remaining true to the school’s rich history. The project involved a logo redesign, a website redesign, the development of a UI & visual style guide and providing art direction. The finished product resulted in a warm, recognisable and prestigious image selling the future vision of the school and driving enrolments through the website.

The project involved implementing the new brand identity and redesigning the school’s website, creating collateral, producing printed marketing material such as flyers, brochures and pull-ups and digital marketing materials such as social media banners and email marketing newsletters.
